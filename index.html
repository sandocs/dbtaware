Understanding DBT Enabled Accounts

Learn the crucial difference between Aadhaar linked and DBT enabled accounts to ensure seamless scholarship disbursements

Aadhaar Linked vs DBT Enabled Accounts

Understanding this difference is crucial for receiving your scholarship payments without delays

Aadhaar Linked Account

Basic linkage only

Aadhaar number is registered with bank account

Cannot receive government scholarships directly

May cause delays in scholarship disbursement

Basic KYC verification only

❌ Not sufficient for scholarship payments

DBT Enabled Account

Scholarship ready

Aadhaar seeded with special DBT configuration

Can receive government benefits directly

Ensures timely scholarship disbursement

Enhanced security and verification

✅ Required for scholarship payments

Important Note

All Pre-Matric and Post-Matric scholarships for SC students are disbursed ONLY through DBT enabled Aadhaar seeded accounts

How to Enable DBT on Your Account

Follow these simple steps to ensure your account can receive scholarship payments

1

Visit Your Bank Branch

Go to the branch where you have your savings account

  • Carry original Aadhaar card
  • Bring your bank passbook
  • Carry a recent passport-size photograph
2

Request DBT Enablement

Ask the bank officer to enable DBT on your Aadhaar seeded account

  • Fill the DBT enablement form
  • Provide updated KYC documents if required
  • Ensure your mobile number is registered
3

Verification Process

Bank will verify your documents and process the request

  • Biometric verification may be required
  • Bank will update your account status
  • You'll receive SMS confirmation
4

Confirmation

Your account is now DBT enabled and ready for scholarships

  • Check your account status online
  • Save the confirmation receipt
  • Update your scholarship applications

Important Tips

  • • Complete this process at least 30 days before scholarship application deadlines
  • • Keep all receipts and confirmation documents safe
  • • Verify your account status before applying for scholarships
  • • Contact your bank if the process takes longer than 15 working days

Scholarship Schemes Information

Learn about the Pre-Matric and Post-Matric scholarship schemes for SC students

Pre-Matric Scholarships

For Classes I to X

Eligibility:

  • • Students belonging to Scheduled Caste category
  • • Studying in Classes I to X
  • • Annual family income below specified limits
  • • Must have DBT enabled bank account

Benefits:

  • • Tuition fee reimbursement
  • • Maintenance allowance
  • • Additional allowances for books and stationery
  • • Direct transfer to student's account

Post-Matric Scholarships

For Classes XI and above

Eligibility:

  • • Students belonging to Scheduled Caste category
  • • Studying in Classes XI and above
  • • Annual family income below specified limits
  • • Must have DBT enabled bank account

Benefits:

  • • Full tuition fee coverage
  • • Maintenance allowance
  • • Study tour and thesis allowances
  • • Higher rates for professional courses
